Scope

Research Gap

Current mobile applications for OCD management often lack features like personalized medication reminders, automated task monitoring, and comprehensive community support. Many existing solutions do not allow integrated features like task tracking and detailed medication reporting, highlighting the need for a more holistic approach.

Research Problem

OCD patients in Sri Lanka face challenges in accessing specialized mental health services, leading to delayed treatment and limited support. The absence of integrated support systems for task tracking and symptom management further exacerbates these issues, affecting the quality of life of individuals with OCD.

Research Objectives

This study aims to develop a comprehensive mobile application that provides therapeutic support for OCD patients, incorporating features like real-time task extraction, personalized medication reminders, and an interactive platform for symptom monitoring. The objective is to create a more accessible and effective support system.

Background

Obsessive-Compulsive Disorder (OCD) is a chronic mental health disorder with intrusive thoughts and repetitive behaviors. In Sri Lanka, many individuals with OCD avoid seeking help due to limited access to specialized services. Mobile health applications have shown potential in bridging this gap by providing therapeutic support.

Final Outcome

Therapeutic Progress Tracker

This feature enables patients to schedule sessions with doctors. Once both parties agree, the session is automatically scheduled and starts within the app. During the session, a transcript is created, extracting tasks for the patient, names of medications, and the next session date. The extracted tasks are added to a to-do list, medication details provide helpful insights, and future sessions are scheduled in the app's calendar.

Creative Wellness Hub

This platform allows users to share experiences with a supportive community. The app recommends posts based on similar conditions, enabling users to connect and receive feedback. Patients can share daily updates with doctors through notes, which the app can turn into editable posts, attach media, and allow others to comment and share their perspectives.

YALE-BROWN OCD Scale

This feature evaluates users using the Yale-Brown Obsessive Compulsive Scale, offering stress-free assessments. Results are recorded and shared with doctors for additional insights. This allows doctors to track the patient's progress in a structured manner, providing necessary support as needed.

Urges & Fears Management

Starting with a chat with the AI chatbot, this feature gathers information on the patient's condition, sending the report to a doctor who recommends actions. For example, for phobias like excessive handwashing, the app provides gentle reminders, using location and prior data to help minimize unnecessary symptoms.
